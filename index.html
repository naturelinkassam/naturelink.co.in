About Us

Nature Care Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to the protection, conservation, and restoration of the natural environment. We work with communities, volunteers, and partners to promote sustainable living and protect biodiversity.

Our Work

Tree Plantation

Organizing tree plantation drives to restore green cover and fight climate change.

Wildlife Protection

Working to protect wildlife habitats and raise awareness about biodiversity.

Clean Environment

Conducting cleanliness drives and promoting waste reduction and recycling.
